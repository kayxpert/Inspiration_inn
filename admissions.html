Eligibility

Who We Support

Our group home serves adults (16 years and older) with disabilities, including but not limited to:

Developmental & Intellectual Disabilities

Comprehensive support for individuals with cognitive and developmental needs.

Autism Spectrum Disorders

Specialized care and structured support for adults on the autism spectrum.

Physical or Neurological Disabilities

Accessible facilities and tailored care for physical and neurological needs.

Other Support Needs

Other conditions requiring supervised residential care in a group setting.
